hunting-gathering

hunting/gathering

the process of aggregating an element that has been marked as aggregate by use of the part= attribute on each of the partial elements (in TEI, the part= attribute is a legal attribute of <l>, <lg>, <div> (including all the <lgN> and <divN> elements), <c>, <cl>, <m>, <phr>, <s>, <seg>, and <w>)

[(Bauman, 2005) p 7-8]
